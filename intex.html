Luca Girardi

Ph.D. Candidate in Aerial Robotics, ETH Zürich

I am a Ph.D. candidate in robotics at ETH Zürich, where I design and control soft, morphing aerial robots inspired by birds and bats. My research brings together structural mechanics, control, and machine learning to create collision-resilient multirotors that can safely navigate cluttered, natural environments.

I am particularly interested in applications to search and rescue, ecological monitoring, and infrastructure inspection. I enjoy collaborating with people from very different backgrounds – many of the best ideas emerge at the intersection of fields that rarely meet.
